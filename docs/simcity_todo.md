# SimCity Plugin Expansion Roadmap (SC4 Inspired)

## Phase 1: Environmental Systems & Desirability [CURRENT]
- [ ] **Air Pollution**: Industrial zones and traffic generate air pollution. Trees and parks mitigate it.
- [ ] **Water Pollution**: Agricultural and dirty industry pollute the water table.
- [ ] **Desirability Map**: Combine pollution, infrastructure, and services into a "desirability" score per tile.

## Phase 2: Density & Wealth Levels
- [ ] **Zoning Densities**: Low, Medium, and High density for RCI zones.
- [ ] **Wealth Tiers**: Residents and buildings categorized into $, $$, and $$$ levels.
- [ ] **Progression Logic**: High density and high wealth require water, power, and high desirability.

## Phase 3: Social Services & Education
- [ ] **Education System**: Implement Schools and Colleges. Track "Education Quotient" (EQ) for residents.
- [ ] **High-Tech Industry**: Clean industry that requires high EQ and low pollution to grow.
- [ ] **Health System**: Hospitals and life expectancy tracking.

## Phase 4: Waste Management
- [ ] **Garbage Generation**: Every building produces garbage.
- [ ] **Landfills & Incinerators**: Required to manage waste.
- [ ] **Sanitation Impact**: Unmanaged garbage drops desirability and health.

## Phase 5: Transportation Diversity
- [ ] **Public Transit**: Bus stops and their impact on traffic reduction.
- [ ] **Traffic Noise**: High traffic volume creates noise pollution, affecting residential areas.
